Should schools require uniforms?

Isaiah: Pro Uniforms

I think schools should require uniforms. The reasons for that are because it creates unity, prepares students for a professional workplace and removes inappropiate wardrobe issues.

First of all, students wearing uniforms creates unity beacuse everyone will look the same and be equal.

Second of all, students wearing uniforms will prapare students for professional workplace. Students will be used to dressing formal and will be more likely to be hired.

Lastly, students wearing uniforms will remove inappropriate wardrobe issues. There will be no need to enforce a dress code and worry about what students wear.
